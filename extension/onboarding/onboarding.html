Welcome to Define & Translate

AI-powered contextual definitions and translations for any text you select on the web.

Before you get started

To provide the service, this extension collects the following data. Please review it before continuing.

Selected text and surrounding context

The text you select and its surrounding text (together up to 300 characters in total) is sent to a third-party AI service to generate the definition and translation. It is your responsibility to ensure no personal and confidential information is selected or in the surrounding text.

Anonymous installation ID & IP address

A random installation ID and your IP address are used for rate-limiting, usage analytics, and troubleshooting. No raw IP address is stored and it is retained for 15 days.

By clicking Get Started, you agree to this data collection as described in our Privacy Policy.

Sorry to see you go

The extension will not collect any data. To remove it from Chrome, please follow one of the steps below.

1

Right-click the Define & Translate icon in the Chrome toolbar, then select "Remove from Chrome"

or

2

Go to chrome://extensions, find Define & Translate, and click "Remove"
